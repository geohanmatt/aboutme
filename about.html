Who Am I?

I'm Geohan, a tech enthusiast and an aspiring developer from the Philippines who finds comfort in solitude, code, and creating things that speak without words.

I was born in the province of Guimaras and have been visually impaired ever since, but that hasn't stopped me from learning, exploring, or doing what I'm passionate about.

This site is my digital corner, built from ideas, late-night thoughts, and a quiet passion for learning and growth.
